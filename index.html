Why VARS?

Built specifically for UAE property management companies. Not another generic tool.

01

Three Apps, One Platform

PMC managers, security guards, and residents each get their own dedicated app — all synced in real time. No more juggling separate systems.

02

Built for the UAE

Designed around local regulations, Arabic language support, and the specific workflows of UAE property management companies.

03

End-to-End Security

Guard shift management, visitor pre-registration, digital check-in, and a complete audit trail. Full control from gate to unit.

Data
Security

End-to-end encrypted and fully compliant with UAE data protection law — your building data, secured.

01
End-to-End Encryption
AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit. Bank-grade protection for all data.
02
UAE Data Protection Law
Fully aligned with UAE PDPL (Federal Decree-Law No. 45/2021). Data hosted on UAE infrastructure.
03
Role-Based Access Control
Granular permissions with every action logged by user, timestamp, and IP.
04
Visitor Logs & Audit Trail
Encrypted visitor records with entry/exit times and resident approval history.
05
Maintenance Records
Tamper-proof work orders and incident logs with digital signatures and automated retention.
