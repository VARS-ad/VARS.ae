What VARS covers.

Occupant Requests
Tenants and staff raise issues from a phone — photo, location, category, urgency
Every request lands in the queue with the right context. No more WhatsApp triage.
Work Orders
Intake, dispatch, status, and close-out — one queue across every trade and contractor
Coordinators see what's open, who's on it, and how long it's been. No spreadsheets.
Planned Preventive Maintenance
Calendar-driven recurring tasks against assets — generated, assigned, and tracked automatically
PPM compliance you can prove. Auditable evidence for every visit.
Asset Register
Every chiller, lift, pump, and panel — location, warranty, service history
One source of truth. New work orders link to the asset, not just an address.
FM Analytics
MTTR, first-time fix rate, PPM compliance, open-WO aging, SLA breaches
Spot patterns across buildings before they become escalations.
System Integration
API-ready for existing ERPs and CMMS. CSV upload for day one
No rip-and-replace. Works alongside what you already use.
